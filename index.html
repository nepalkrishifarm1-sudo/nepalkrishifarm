Our Story

Rooted in Jhapa,
Growing for Nepal

Nepal Krishi Tatha Pasupanchi Farm was founded with a single vision — to show that Nepal's fertile land can produce world-class organic pineapples, while creating meaningful employment for local communities. Located in Bhadrapur, Jhapa, we use only organic materials: compost, natural fertilizers, and sustainable farming methods that protect the environment.

The Man Behind the Vision

Manoj Singh
Founder · Leading Agricultural Entrepreneur & Pineapple Pioneer, Jhapa, Nepal

Manoj Singh is a prominent agricultural entrepreneur from Jyamirgadi-1, Jhapa, recognized as one of Nepal's most successful and largest-scale pineapple farmers. Born in 2036 BS into a large family facing severe financial hardships, Manoj overcame early adversity through sheer resilience and vision. What began as a modest agricultural endeavor has blossomed into a multi-million rupee commercial farming enterprise, inspiring a generational shift toward modern agriculture in eastern Nepal.

Humble Beginnings

Raised alongside seven siblings, Manoj completed his education up to the secondary level under difficult financial conditions.

The Turning Point (2060/61 BS)

His commercial farming journey officially began with just 7,000 pineapple saplings gifted to him by his father-in-law.

Scale of Operations

Today, his core pineapple plantation alone holds over 500,000+ plants spanning 28–30 bighas of land.

Diversification

Beyond pineapples, Manoj has scaled into tea plantation management, leasing roughly 90 bighas in Bhadrapur and employing 100–150 local workers daily.

Impact & Philosophy

With a net annual profit of 20–22 Lakhs NPR from pineapples alone and an overall enterprise valuation running into crores, Manoj stands as a testament to the profitability of dedicated commercial farming in Nepal. Despite his immense success, he remains deeply connected to his roots.

"If I had chosen any other profession, achieving my current status would have been impossible. I am fully content, happy, and see my agricultural profession as my true fortune."
— Manoj Singh

His son, Raju Singh Koiree, now carries this legacy forward — modernizing the farm's operations for a new generation.

Mr. Raju Singh Koiree
Leadership
Mr. Raju Singh Koiree
Managing Director · Nepal Krishi Tatha Pasupanchi Farm

A young and passionate entrepreneur from Nepal, Raju Singh Koiree is managing and directing Nepal Krishi Tatha Pasupanchi Farm with a belief that Nepal's youth need not cross borders to find opportunity. Son of founder Manoj Singh, Raju now carries the family legacy forward, modernizing the farm's operations with fresh ideas and technology. With deep roots in Jhapa and a bold vision for Nepal's agricultural future, he has built a model farm that not only produces premium organic pineapples but also demonstrates that world-class work can happen right here at home.

"I started this farm to prove one thing: Nepal has everything it takes. Our land is fertile, our people are skilled, and our pineapples are second to none. Why go abroad when our country needs us to build it here?"
— Raju Singh Koiree
